Place your video files here to use in the marketplace course previews.

Required files:
- course_preview_demo.mp4: This is the demo video used for course previews.

For development and testing, you'll need to add actual video files to this directory.
The app is configured to look for videos in this location.

Video format requirements:
- MP4 format with H.264 encoding is recommended for maximum compatibility
- Keep file sizes reasonable (under 10MB) to avoid performance issues
- Dimensions should be 16:9 aspect ratio for best display in the video player
- Resolution of 720p or lower is sufficient for mobile devices
